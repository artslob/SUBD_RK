Question: 27

You are using TISPITR to recover one or more tablespaces to a previous time in point or back to a specific SCN. What included in the recovery set? (Choose all that apply.)

Answer: E

Explanation: The recovery set includes only the datafiles from the tablespaces to be recovered. A, B, C, and D are incorrect. None of these objects is included in the recovery set; instead, all of them are included except for the UNDO tablespace .
